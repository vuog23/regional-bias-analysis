# Screening Criteria

## Include Papers If They Are About

- regional or geographic bias
- dataset bias
- visual domain shift
- object-recognition robustness
- fairness or worst-group performance in vision
- GeoDE
- Dollar Street
- ImageNet bias
- CLIP or SigLIP robustness or bias
- DINOv2, MAE, or self-supervised robustness
- domain generalization

## Exclude Papers If They Are

- only about NLP bias with no vision relevance
- only about medical imaging unless the method is directly relevant
- only about generative image models with no evaluation relevance
- not connected to geographic, domain, dataset bias, or visual robustness

## Evidence Rules

- Do not invent citations.
- Do not invent paper findings.
- Do not summarize papers from memory if they have not been found or provided.
- Use TODO: evidence needed when a claim lacks a source.
- Prefer primary paper pages, PDFs, OpenReview entries, proceedings pages, arXiv pages, and official project pages.
