# Prompt: Draft Related Work

Draft the Related Work section using only verified literature sources.

Allowed sources:

- `paper/references.bib`
- `literature/bibtex_raw.bib`
- `literature/paper_inventory.csv`
- `literature/related_work_matrix.csv`
- `literature/citation_bank.md`
- `literature/claim_bank.md`
- `notes/paper_summaries/`

Rules:

- Do not cite a paper unless its key exists in the BibTeX files.
- Do not summarize a paper from memory if it has not been found or provided.
- Do not invent paper findings.
- Use `TODO: evidence needed` for missing claims.
- Use cautious language.

Organize around:

- geographic bias in computer vision
- dataset bias and domain shift
- GeoDE and Dollar Street
- vision-language model bias and robustness
- self-supervised model robustness
- regional fairness metrics and worst-group evaluation
