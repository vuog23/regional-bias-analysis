# Prompt: Draft Discussion

Draft the Analysis and Discussion section using validated results and verified literature.

Rules:

- Do not claim causality unless directly supported.
- Use cautious language: suggests, indicates, may reflect, is consistent with, may be associated with.
- Do not invent mechanisms.
- Explicitly separate observed results from possible interpretations.
- Use `TODO: evidence needed` when evidence is missing.

Discuss:

- why finetuning improves or does not improve regional bias gap
- why some models may have higher accuracy but also higher regional gap
- differences between CNN, transformer, self-supervised, and vision-language models
- dataset-specific behavior between GeoDE and Dollar Street
- how findings relate to the literature
- limitations of the metric and available result files
